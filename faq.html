Everything you need to know about AudioLuxe Elite

Simply open the charging case near your device with Bluetooth enabled, and a pop-up will appear on your screen asking to connect. For manual connection, go to your device's Bluetooth settings and select "AudioLuxe Elite" from the list of available devices.

Yes, you can use either the left or right earbud independently. Simply take out the earbud you want to use from the case, and it will automatically connect to your device. The other earbud can remain in the charging case.

Yes, the AudioLuxe Elite charging case supports both USB-C wired charging and Qi-certified wireless charging. You can place the case on any wireless charging pad to charge it.

The AudioLuxe Elite package includes: the earbuds, a charging case, a USB-C charging cable, three sizes of silicone ear tips (S, M, L), a quick start guide, and a warranty card.

AudioLuxe Elite features intuitive touch controls. Single tap to play/pause music or answer/end calls. Double tap to skip to the next track. Triple tap to go to the previous track. Long press to activate voice assistant or switch between ANC and transparency mode.
